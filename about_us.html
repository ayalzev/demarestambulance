Who Are We?

From car accidents to cardiac arrents, falls to fainting, difficulty breathing to diabetic emergencies, DVAC is ready to respond with professionalism and dedication. We are a small group of dedicated volunteers who serve Demarest and surrounding towns every hour or every day. All of our members are certified in their specialty, whether it be Ambulance Driving or State Licesnsed Emergency Medical Technicians (EMTs). Most of us are from Demarest, though we have a good core who are from neighboring towns.
