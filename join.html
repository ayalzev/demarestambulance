We Need Your Help!

The ambulance corps is being run by a small group of people, who give more than double the amount of time that is needed for this corps to function, WE NEED MORE HELP. If you ever wanted to give back to your community, be able to help someone in their time of need, help a scared child, then we are looking for you. We know what you're saying - "I can't do it" - but you CAN, and we need you.

We Are Looking For

  • Students 16 years or older
  • At home moms and dads
  • Retirees or anyone who can donate a few hours in the day
